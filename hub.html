Trust & safety online

Don't get hurt twice.

Returning citizens are heavily targeted by scammers. These tools cut the risk most.

Lock down identity

Free credit freeze (3 bureaus)

Freezing your credit is free, takes 5 minutes per bureau, and stops new accounts being opened in your name. Unfreeze when you actually apply for credit.

Password manager

Use a different strong password for every account. Bitwarden is free forever; 1Password has a paid tier with family sharing.

2FA / passkeys

Google's 2-step block 99.9% of automated attacks. Use authenticator apps, not SMS where possible.
