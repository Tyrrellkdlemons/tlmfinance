Privacy Policy

The Last Mile · Finance is a free, donor-funded reentry tool. We collect the absolute minimum needed to help you save your plan and contact you when you ask us to.

Last updated:

What we collect

If you don't sign in: nothing leaves your device. Your reentry plan is stored in your browser's local storage. We don't run analytics on your visit.

If you sign in with email, Google, Apple, or Facebook:

  • Your email address (and name + profile picture if your provider returns them)
  • Which provider you used (Google / Apple / Facebook / email)
  • The date you signed in last and signed up
  • Your reentry plan content, if you choose to save it across devices
  • Whether you opted in to the optional weekly newsletter

If you opt in to the newsletter we keep your email so we can send you the weekly digest. You can unsubscribe at any time from the link inside any email or from your account menu on the site.

Where it lives

User data is stored in our Supabase project (Postgres database, hosted in the U.S.) with row-level security so each row is only readable by the account that owns it. The site itself is hosted on Netlify. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone other than these processors and only to the extent needed to run the site.

What we don't do

  • We don't sell your data.
  • We don't show ads.
  • We don't track you across other sites.
  • We don't profile or score you.

How to delete your data

See the data-deletion page — same info, single instruction list.

Children

The site is not directed at children under 13 and we don't knowingly collect their data. If you believe we have, email tyrrellkdlemons@gmail.com and we'll remove it.

Changes

If we change this policy materially, we'll update the date above and post a banner on the site for a week.

Contact

Questions, requests, deletions, complaints — reply to any email from us, or reach tyrrellkdlemons@gmail.com.
