From the inside out

Education. Reentry. Employment.

The Last Mile model in three acts. Finance is the throughline that makes each act stick — which is why this companion exists.

01
Education

Skills built inside.

Full-scale tech labs in correctional facilities — secure internet, live remote instruction, web development, audio/video production, project management, sales, entrepreneurship, AI literacy.

02
Reentry

72-hour to 90-day plan.

The first three months decide a lot. Money, documents, housing, transit, health, family, and a job lead. Plus financial literacy that respects where you've been.

03
Employment

Career, not just a paycheck.

Apprenticeships, internships, and direct hires at major employers — plus continuing education that lets you stack credentials over years, not months.
