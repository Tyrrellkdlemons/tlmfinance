Education after release

Project Rebound — a college pathway.

Project Rebound supports formerly incarcerated students at California State University campuses. CSUF Project Rebound has supported 300+ students since 2016 and offers scholarships, mentoring, the John Irwin Memorial House, and harm-reduction workshops.

Visit CSUF Project Rebound → CSU System

What's offered

  • Scholarships and academic resources
  • Customized mentoring and tutoring
  • Career placement and academic counseling
  • The John Irwin Memorial House (reentry housing)
  • Restorative justice circles and harm-reduction workshops

Eligibility: anyone impacted by the criminal justice system at any level.
