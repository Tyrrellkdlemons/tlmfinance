About the show

A platform for reform and personal change.

The Last Mile Radio amplifies stories of criminal-justice reform, second chances, and the transformative potential of education and employment after incarceration. Episodes feature alumni, founders, scholars, and changemakers.

Current host: Tara Trask

Live broadcast: Saturdays @ 12pm ET on SiriusXM
