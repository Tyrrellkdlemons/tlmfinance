# How to Use Pathways Home - Step-by-Step Guide

## Overview

**Pathways Home** is a 2-year program that helps participants achieve housing independence and financial stability through quarterly milestones, coaching support, and financial planning tools.

---

## 🚪 Step 1: Access Pathways Home

### From the Main Site (index.html)
1. Go to http://localhost:8080 (or https://tlmfinance.netlify.app in production)
2. Look for the **"Pathways Home"** link in the top navigation bar
   - It's located between "Plan" and "Learn"
   - Styled in gold to stand out
3. Click "Pathways Home" to enter the program

### Direct Access
Navigate directly to:
```
http://localhost:8080/pathways.html
```

---

## 🔐 Step 2: Sign In

**Note**: You must sign in to use Pathways Home features

1. On the Pathways Home page, click **"Sign In"** in the top-right corner
2. Choose your sign-in method:
   - **Google** (recommended)
   - **Apple**
   - **Facebook**
   - **Email + Password**
3. Complete the authentication flow
4. You'll be redirected back to Pathways Home, now signed in

**First-time users**: If you don't have an account, click "Create account" instead

---

## 📝 Step 3: Enroll in the Program

After signing in for the first time, you need to complete the enrollment process.

### Enrollment Wizard (4 Steps)

#### Step 1: Personal Information
- **Name**: Your full name
- **Phone**: Contact number (optional but recommended)
- **Release Date**: When you were released from incarceration
- **Address**: Current housing address

#### Step 2: Current Situation
Answer questions about:
- Current housing status (e.g., transitional housing, with family, shelter, etc.)
- Employment status
- Primary challenges you're facing

#### Step 3: Program Goals
Select what you hope to achieve through Pathways Home:
- [ ] Secure stable housing
- [ ] Gain employment
- [ ] Improve financial literacy
- [ ] Build credit
- [ ] Save for long-term goals
- [ ] Develop life skills
- [ ] Other (specify)

#### Step 4: Consent & Commitment
Review the program overview:
- **Duration**: 2 years (8 quarters)
- **Components**:
  - Quarterly check-ins (every 3 months)
  - Milestone tracking
  - Financial planning tools
  - Coach support
  - Resource library

- [ ] **Check the consent box** to acknowledge you've read the program details
- Click **"Complete Enrollment"**

**Success!** You're now enrolled in Pathways Home 🎉

---

## 📊 Step 4: Explore Your Dashboard

After enrolling, you'll land on your **Dashboard** - your home base in Pathways Home.

### Dashboard Components

#### 1. Hero Section (Top)
- **Welcome message** with your name
- **Program statistics**:
  - Days in program
  - Current quarter (e.g., "Quarter 2 of 8")
  - Overall progress percentage

#### 2. Current Quarter Focus
- Shows which quarter you're in
- Lists the **focus area** for this quarter:
  - Q1: Stabilization
  - Q2: Skill Building
  - Q3: Goal Setting
  - Q4: Independence Preparation
  - (Repeats for Year 2)

#### 3. Active Milestones
Interactive checklist of milestones for the current quarter:
- [ ] Example: "Complete housing application"
- [ ] Example: "Open a savings account"
- [ ] Example: "Attend financial literacy workshop"

**How to use**:
- Click the checkbox to mark a milestone as complete
- Changes save automatically to the database
- Coach can see your progress in real-time

#### 4. Financial Plan Card
Quick access to your financial plan:
- View your plan
- Edit your plan
- Create a new plan

#### 5. Program Timeline
Collapsible view showing all 8 quarters:
- Expand/collapse with "Show/Hide Timeline"
- See past, current, and future quarters
- View completed milestones and check-in dates

#### 6. Quick Actions
- **Schedule Coach Meeting** - Book time with your coach
- **Complete Check-In** - Quarterly progress form
- **View Resources** - Educational materials

---

## ✅ Step 5: Complete Quarterly Check-Ins

Every 3 months, you'll complete a check-in to track your progress.

### When to Check In
- End of each quarter (months 3, 6, 9, 12, 15, 18, 21, 24)
- Dashboard will show a notification when it's time
- Coach may send a reminder

### Check-In Process
1. Click **"Complete Check-In"** on your dashboard
2. Answer ~15 questions about:
   - Housing stability
   - Employment status
   - Financial situation
   - Goal progress
   - Challenges encountered
   - Support needs
3. Review and submit
4. Coach will review and schedule a follow-up meeting

---

## 💰 Step 6: Build Your Financial Plan

Pathways Home includes a comprehensive financial planning tool.

### Plan Sections (15 total)
1. **Income Sources** - Job, benefits, side income
2. **Monthly Expenses** - Rent, food, transportation, etc.
3. **Debts** - Credit cards, loans, court fees
4. **Savings Goals** - Emergency fund, future housing
5. **Credit Building** - Steps to improve credit score
6. **Banking** - Checking/savings accounts
7. **Benefits** - SNAP, Medicaid, housing vouchers
8. **Employment** - Current job, career goals
9. **Education** - Training, certifications
10. **Transportation** - Car, bus pass, bike
11. **Healthcare** - Insurance, medications
12. **Family Support** - Dependents, child support
13. **Legal** - Probation fees, restitution
14. **Emergency Contacts** - Who to call in crisis
15. **Long-term Vision** - 5-year goals

### How to Use
1. Go to **Dashboard** → **Financial Plan** → **"Create New Plan"**
2. Fill out each section (save as you go)
3. Review and edit anytime
4. Share with your coach during meetings
5. Update quarterly as your situation changes

---

## 🎯 Step 7: Track Milestones

Milestones are specific, achievable goals for each quarter.

### Milestone Examples

**Quarter 1: Stabilization**
- [ ] Secure stable housing
- [ ] Obtain ID/driver's license
- [ ] Open bank account
- [ ] Apply for benefits (SNAP, Medicaid)
- [ ] Complete intake with coach

**Quarter 2: Skill Building**
- [ ] Attend financial literacy workshop
- [ ] Create monthly budget
- [ ] Start building credit (secured card)
- [ ] Update resume
- [ ] Join job training program

**Quarter 3: Goal Setting**
- [ ] Set 6-month savings goal
- [ ] Research career pathways
- [ ] Enroll in education/training
- [ ] Build emergency fund ($500)
- [ ] Improve credit score by 20 points

**Quarter 4: Independence Preparation**
- [ ] Apply for permanent housing
- [ ] Secure full-time employment
- [ ] Save first/last month rent
- [ ] Complete life skills training
- [ ] Develop transition plan

### Completing Milestones
1. View milestones on your dashboard
2. Click checkbox to mark complete
3. Coach verifies completion
4. Celebrate your progress! 🎉

---

## 👥 Step 8: Work with Your Coach

Your coach is your guide through the Pathways Home journey.

### Coach Responsibilities
- Review quarterly check-ins
- Track milestone completion
- Provide accountability and support
- Connect you to resources
- Help troubleshoot challenges
- Celebrate your wins

### How to Communicate
- **Messages** - In-app messaging (coming soon)
- **Meetings** - Schedule via dashboard
- **Check-ins** - Quarterly forms
- **Phone/Email** - Contact info in profile

### Meeting Preparation
Before each coach meeting:
1. Complete your quarterly check-in
2. Update milestone progress
3. Review financial plan
4. Write down questions/concerns
5. Bring supporting documents if needed

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What if I miss a quarterly check-in?
**A**: Complete it as soon as you can. Your coach will be notified and can help you catch up.

### Q: Can I change my milestones?
**A**: Talk to your coach. Milestones are customizable based on your situation.

### Q: Is my data private?
**A**: Yes! Only you, your coach, and program admins can see your data. See Privacy Policy for details.

### Q: What happens after 2 years?
**A**: You'll graduate from Pathways Home! You can continue using the financial planning tools indefinitely.

### Q: Can I pause the program?
**A**: Discuss with your coach. Short breaks are OK, but staying consistent leads to better outcomes.

### Q: What if I need immediate help?
**A**: Contact your coach ASAP. For emergencies, call 988 (crisis) or 911.

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

### Problem: Can't find the Pathways Home link
**Solution**:
- Make sure you're signed in (it may be hidden when logged out)
- Look in the top navigation between "Plan" and "Learn"
- Try refreshing the page
- Use direct link: http://localhost:8080/pathways.html

### Problem: Enrollment wizard won't submit
**Solution**:
- Make sure all required fields are filled
- Check internet connection
- Try refreshing and starting over
- Contact support if issue persists

### Problem: Milestones not saving
**Solution**:
- Check internet connection
- Refresh page and try again
- Clear browser cache
- Make sure you're still signed in

### Problem: Can't access dashboard
**Solution**:
- Verify you're signed in
- Confirm you've completed enrollment
- Check URL: should be `pathways.html#/dashboard`
- Try signing out and back in
