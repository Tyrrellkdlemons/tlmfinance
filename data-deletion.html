Delete my data

You can delete your TLM Finance account and every bit of data tied to it (including any info Facebook Login passed us) using one of three methods below.

Option 1 — One-click in your account menu

  1. Open tlmfinance.netlify.app on the device you signed in from
  2. Click the avatar / "Account" pill in the top-right (or the Account tab in the bottom nav on mobile)
  3. Choose Delete my account
  4. Confirm — your account, plan, audit log, and newsletter subscription are wiped within seconds

Option 2 — Email request

Email tyrrellkdlemons@gmail.com from the email address you used to sign in (or include enough info to identify your account). We will:

  • Confirm receipt within 48 hours
  • Delete your account and every related row from our Supabase database within 7 days
  • Send you a confirmation when done

Option 3 — Phone

Call or text 323-972-6100. Leave your sign-in email or the name on the account and we'll process it within 7 days.

What gets deleted

  • Your authentication record (email, provider, name, avatar URL)
  • Your reentry plan content (every section)
  • Your audit-log events on our server
  • Your newsletter subscription, if any
  • Any local data stored in your browser when you visit while signed out

Anything sourced from Facebook Login (your name, email, profile picture URL) is removed at the same time. We retain no derivative copies, no backups beyond standard 30-day rolling Supabase backups, and we don't sell or share any of it before deletion.

Confirmation code

Once your data is deleted we email you a unique confirmation code you can show Facebook (Facebook requires this for apps that use Facebook Login). The code is also visible inside your final confirmation email's footer.
